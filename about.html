About Us

30+ Years of Trusted Scaffolding

Founded by David Fisher in 1995, Altitude Scaffolding has grown to become Cape Town's premier scaffolding rental and hire service — trusted by film studios, construction firms, and event organisers.

Our Story

Built Safe. Built Strong.

Since 1995, Altitude Scaffolding has been the backbone of some of Cape Town's most ambitious projects. Founded by David Paul Fisher, we've grown into the city's most trusted name in scaffolding for film productions, construction, and events.

With an excellent safety record over 31 years and ISO 9001:2015 certification, we deliver peace of mind on every job. David brings over 50 years of scaffolding experience — beginning in the UK in 1975 and across South Africa since 1983 — built on German-engineered Layher systems and a zero-compromise approach to safety.

  • 150+ feature films and adverts supported
  • 2000+ construction and event projects
  • Zero safety incidents since 1995
  • FEM Awards — 1st place 2024, 2nd place 2025
